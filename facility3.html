THE ANNAPURNA EXPRESS DETAILS

The Annapurna Express is a registered tour and travel agency under the flagship of The Annapurna Limited, owning company of The Annapurna Resort & Spa, Pokhara. With a main office co-located with the Resort marketing office in Kathmandu, it has a travel desk in the lobby at the resort in Pokhara. Both are able to assist and make arrangements for all travel, trek, river running, jungle lodge, Kathmandu hotel, and other reservation needs from the moment of first arrival in Nepal, to final departure.
